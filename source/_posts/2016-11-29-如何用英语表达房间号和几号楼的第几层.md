一般按照房间号、楼层号，和楼号的顺序写：
```
Room ***, 1st(2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th....) Floor, 1st(2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th....) Biulding.
```
简写：
```
Room ***, 1st(2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th....) F, 1st(2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th....) Bld.
```
